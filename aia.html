AUDIENCE ISSUES, CONCERNS, NEEDS APPROACH

  • This group will actively comment, engage & upload their own favorite recipes
  • Needs to be fun!
  • Easy to read, navigate, print, download recipes
  • Should have relaxed social vibe and page flow to encourage use by older relatives & friends
  • Needs to develop a catchy logo
Social Media Network: Ages ~20s-60s, moderate-skilled pc/phone/laptop users. Lots of friends & friends-of-friends interact with client’s online FACEBOOK page & PINTEREST
  • Client currently posts food & beverage recipes & short personal cooking vids that are popular & widely shared
  • Client is also a known local musician
  • Add links to showcase his band and music
  • Prefers a community-oriented lifestyle type of page, lively, graphically interesting
Food & Beverage, Music Industry Coworkers: ~20’-50s, skilled pc, phone, tablet, laptop users. Can be a potential network for hiring client’s services
  • This group can be more ‘content critical’ since they are peer professionals
  • Page needs to present client as a serious potential hire for freelance catering services
  • Recipe data, ingredients, measurements, etc
  • An about section highlighting client’s skills
  • Contact form especially helpful
  • *Avail to hiring for catering services
  • Link for live short ‘how to’ cooking videos
  • Consider space for passive adverts of related food and music industry to help monetize site
